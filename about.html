Born from a desire for real immersion.

Founded in 2023 by a group of former electrical engineers and narrative designers, EscapeX was built to solve a problem: Escape rooms had become boring. Box with a combination lock? Not here.

We use complex arrays of hidden sensors, localized microcontrollers, and atmospheric effects to ensure you forget you're playing a game.
