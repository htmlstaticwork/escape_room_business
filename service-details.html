The Reactor Core

Success Rate: 18%. Mandatory Team Split.

Mission Briefing

Facility 7's central reactor has initiate a self-destruct meltdown sequence following an unidentified sabotage event. The primary blast doors have sealed entirely.

Upon entry, your team will be physically separated. Group A will manage the manual override in the Control Room. Group B will navigate the dark, irradiated Processing sector. You cannot see each other. You must relay complex visual symbols and coordinate synchronized button presses via analog radio to halt the core temperature rise.

Critical Dynamics
  • No cellular devices allowed
  • Frequent loud sirens & strobe lighting
  • Live dashboard stat tracking
